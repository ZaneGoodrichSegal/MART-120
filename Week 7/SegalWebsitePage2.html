birthday story


One of these years Id like to
Forget my own birthday
And be hastily reminded
By an email

From a loved one
Or a robot
Sent by a corporation


Id like to run to the store
During my lunch break
Desperate to find something to
Act like I didn't slip between
My own fingers


Amongst other things


Sweating.
Like a father from a comic strip-
Forgetting his anniversary, or valentines day,
And no gifts on Hanukkah,
nor any chocolate eggs for easter
Quickly dispersed in the backyard.


The thought is postponed in lue of work or life.

Id like to wake up completely unaware,
And sit on my front porch drinking coffee.

Baking in the comfort of summer heat
Something you I remember-
Distant memory
Decimated by time
Sobbing and Soaking
The first time I have ever been held
Hot breath over my body
Sweating on my first august morning
